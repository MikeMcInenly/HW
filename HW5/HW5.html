Equivalent plastic strength time series analysis

The rate of plastic deformation in ductile materials, such as metals, is characterized by a scalar value known as the equivalent plastic strength (EQPS). The EQPS scalar measures how a material begins to deform as a function of stress over time in the x,y,z directions.

We can use interactive animations and data filters to visualize this concept. As such, let's take our model of a can being crushed by a block to take it one step further and drill down to the exact pixel in which EQPS is at a maximum.
